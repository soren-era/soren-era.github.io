Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Soren Era content management platform, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Social media account identifiers required to publish content on your behalf (e.g., TikTok user ID, access tokens).
  • Content Data: Videos, captions, hashtags, and scheduling preferences you provide.
  • Usage Data: Posting times, engagement metrics, and platform analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Schedule and publish content to your connected social media accounts.
  • Optimize posting times and content performance.
  • Improve the Service and develop new features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the social media platforms you explicitly connect (TikTok, Instagram, X) for the purpose of publishing your content.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on local systems. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Access tokens are encrypted and stored separately from content data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Disconnect your social media accounts from the Service.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with third-party platforms (TikTok, Instagram, X). Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at @soren.era on TikTok.
